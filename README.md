# Cyber Complexity 

A concept of a work sheet for emergency managers to judge the complexity of a cyber incident and assist in explaining impact.
The idea was borrowed from a FEMA course with an Emergency Management depart that specialized in fighting fires, from wild fires to structures.

#### Goal

The goal is to have a worksheet that can be developed and adapted to the needs of the team/person/company to assist in explaining the complexity of cyber incident in terms the understandable and assist in understanding the impact. Similar to the concept of FEMA/Emergency Management we cannot stop a Hurricane or Natural Disaster but we can choose how we responds. Obviously there are ways to prevent, lessen the impact and work through preventing a cyber incident. In the middle of the event or for the less technical that ship has sailed and now a goal of returning to normal is in play.

#### First Draft

This is one of the first drafts that I have shared, these include assumptions and attempts but please feel free to make changes, and updates as needed. The idea is that this gives you a start but you (anyone who needs this) makes changes that work for you.
